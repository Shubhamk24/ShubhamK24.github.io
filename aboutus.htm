About Us

What is TAdS?

Technology Adventure Society is an initiative by Technology Students’ Gymkhana, IIT Kharagpur to provide a platform for students and faculty members to explore and experience the outdoors beyond the campus. It organizes expeditions covering a host of events such as trekking, rock climbing, mountaineering, rappelling and so on.

What do we do?

We aim to make the absolutely best of the flat terrain around Kharagpur and the vacations we get in a year while having the maximum fun possible. While our stock activities are trekking, rock climbing and mountain biking, we have also organized skiing and river rafting occasionally.

What is our purpose?

To promote the spirit of adventure in the young enthusiastic explorers, cultivating team spirit and the vision to overcome obstacles.

Objectives :

(a)To carry out as many adventure sports related activity in a minimal budget as possible.

(b)To provide high quality adventure activities that leads to personality development and leads to boost in one’s confidence.

(c)To introduce a novice (student or faculty) various aspects (planning, training session, first aid, logistics) of Adventure sports, and training those who were serious about it.

(d)This would also help us in avoiding mishaps and prepare them for any eventualities that might occur during an expedition.

(e)Also it would serve as local hub in the campus for various adventure activities related information and help people to plan their activity in a systematic way right from getting information about the place as well as dos and don’ts on the trip.

How we promote Adventure?

Our society has strong mission to bring awareness among youth regarding adventure sports and at the same time provide them with life changing experience which adventure offers at each step.
